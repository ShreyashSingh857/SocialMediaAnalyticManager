Privacy Policy

This application is a hackathon prototype designed to demonstrate
Facebook Login and Instagram Business analytics functionality.

Data Collection:
The app only accesses data explicitly authorized by the user via
Facebook Login, including Instagram Business account insights
(e.g., views, likes, reach).

Data Usage:
Data is used solely to display analytics to the authenticated user.
The app does not sell, share, or use data for advertising purposes.

Data Storage:
No personal data is stored permanently. Access tokens are used
temporarily for demonstration purposes only.

User Control:
Users can revoke access at any time through their Facebook account
settings.
